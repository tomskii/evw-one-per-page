Get an electronic visa waiver to enter the UK

You can get an electronic visa waiver (EVW) if you have a passport from Kuwait, Oman, Qatar or the United Arab Emirates. You must pay £15 for an EVW.

It lets you stay in the UK for up to 6 months for tourism, business, study or medical care.

If you are visiting for another reason, check if you need a visa instead. You cannot use your EVW to:

  • work in the UK
  • get married or register a civil partnership

Apply for an EVW.

Changes to your travel plans

You must change your travel details on your EVW at least 48 hours before travelling if you will:

  • leave from or arrive at a different airport, port or train station than planned
  • arrive in the UK more than 8 hours later than planned

You will need your EVW number and your date of birth.
