What happens next?

We will check your details before we issue your electronic visa waiver - this can take up to 24 hours.

We will email ali@gmail.com with details of how to download your electronic visa waiver.

Please add electronicvisawaiver@homeoffice.gsi.gov.uk to your safe senders list to make sure you get your download details. You should wait 24 hours before contacting us about your EVW.

Changing your details

Email electronicvisawaiver@homeoffice.gsi.gov.uk if you need to change your electronic visa waiver details, for example if your UK arrival date has changed.

Any changes must be made at least 48 hours before you travel to the UK.
